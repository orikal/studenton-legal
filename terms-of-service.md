## 2. Description of the Service

StudenTeam provides tools for **personal academic organization** and optional **community features**, including:

### 2.1 Academic organizer features

- Managing **courses** (name, code, credits, semester, grades, colors).
- Tracking **exams** (date, time, location/room, notes).
- Managing **assignments** (title, due date, status, course association).
- Recording **grades** (components, weights, scores, dates, weighted averages).
- Scheduling **weekly schedule slots** and **personal events** with optional text-based location fields (classroom/building names, not GPS).
- Tracking **study sessions** (duration, mode, course links).
- **Gamification features** (streaks, points, levels, achievements) to motivate learning.
- Creating **practice questions** and study content (where available).

### 2.2 Community and social features

- Participating in **subject-based forums** organized by academic subjects and courses.
- Publishing and viewing **forum posts** (questions, tips, summaries, resources, discussions) with text, images (PNG/JPG/GIF), and file attachments (PDF, Word, Excel, PowerPoint, etc.).
- Posting **replies** to forum threads with optional file attachments.
- **Reporting** inappropriate content (posts, replies, reviews) for moderation.
- **Subscribing** to forum threads for reply notifications.
- Viewing **tutor reviews** and writing reviews for tutors (if you are a student).

### 2.3 Private tutor marketplace

- Browsing **private tutor listings** — informational posts by tutors offering teaching services, including subject, teaching mode (in-person/online), hourly rate in NIS, and optional CV/certification documents.
- Publishing **tutor listings** (if you register as a private tutor) with descriptive text, pricing, and attachments.
- Viewing **tutor profiles** with reviews, ratings, uploaded documents, and contact options.
- **Connecting with tutors** via in-app direct messaging to discuss lesson arrangements.

Listings are **informational only**. You are **solely responsible** for vetting tutors or students, verifying qualifications, and for any agreements, payments, meetings, or disputes. Use caution when sharing contact details or meeting in person.

### 2.4 Private direct messaging

- **One-to-one conversations** with other users (text, images, file attachments).
- **Blocking users** to prevent them from messaging you.
- **Deleting conversations** and their message history.
- Optional link to a related forum post when starting a conversation from a listing or post.

**Privacy note:** Direct messages are intended to be private between you and the conversation participant, but see Section 6.5 regarding reported content and legal process.

### 2.5 Tutor session coordination (scheduling tool)

- **Proposing lesson times** between students and tutors (date, time, duration, hourly rate in NIS).
- **Confirming, rescheduling, or cancelling** session proposals.
- Viewing **session booking history** and status (pending, confirmed, cancelled, completed).
